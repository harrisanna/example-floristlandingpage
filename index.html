About Us

Vanessa's Flowers was founded by Vanessa Woodrow in 1997. Since then, we have catered to thousands of customers with the upmost satisfactory response.

Our company's mission is to provide a joyful and pleasant experience while shedding just a little bit of light, one bouquet at a time.

"I was never very good at telling others how much they meant to me. Flowers did it for me; they provided me with my love language. When I started my first little shop, my dream was to share with everyone the sentimental pleasure behind one of the most beautiful things our planet has to offer... Flowers!"

- Vanessa Woodrow, Owner
